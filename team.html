Tennis is a team sports. Are you captaining a tennis team or playing on a tennis team? TennisKeeper can help you track your matches, lineups, team performance using your iPhone or iPad. No more adhoc-notes or paper.

Create Tennis Team

Create a tennis team, add your team roster and matches.

View the win, loss stats for each team member any time.

Edit Team Matches

Create the matches for a season by entering the match date and the team you are competing against.

You can add your own notes and insights for each match.

Create Lineup for each Match

Create your match lineup quickly using TennisKeeper. Select who is playing which match line and enter their match scores.

You can enter singles or doubles matches and their scores (e.g. regular set, pro set) easily.
