How are the stats calculated?

TennisKeeper uses the data you entered to compute your stats. The following stats are available:

  1. Daily time spent and calorie count on your tennis activities.
  2. Total and average time spent and calorie burned on your tennis actvities by week, month or year.
  3. Win, loss match stats for last week.
  4. Win, loss match stats grouped by games, sets, matches for any week (Pro Edition).
  5. Lifetime singles and doubles Head to Head match statistics against any opponents (Pro Edition).

To view your stats:

  1. Go to TennisKeeper > Stats.
  2. Tap any of the item.

If you want to refresh your stats, tap Stats > Recalculate Your Stats. TennisKeeper will retrieve your latest data from iCloud and recalculate your stats. This operation may take a few seconds.
