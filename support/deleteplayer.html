How do I delete a player?

  1. Go to TennisKeeper > Players.
  2. Tap the Edit button. Select the player and click Delete. Tap the Done button.

Another way is to use swipe left:

  1. Go to TennisKeeper > Players.
  2. Swipe left on a player, then click Delete.
