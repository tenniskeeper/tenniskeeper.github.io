You can launch TennisKeeper directly from the Siri Watch Face by tapping on its platter. The platter is populated with the same launch parameters as your last session. This makes it easy for users that always use TennisKeeper in the same way.


How to setup Siri Watch Face

You can set up Siri Watch Face directly on Apple Watch or you can use the Watch app from your iPhone:

  1. Launch Watch app on your iPhone.
  2. Tap on Face Gallery.
  3. Scroll down and tap Siri.
  4. Tap Add.

  5. Make sure you set Siri as your current Watch Face.


Launching TennisKeeper from Siri Watch Face

If you are using your Siri Watch Face, Siri will overtime learn what apps you use and what data you need and present them to you at the right time. From a TennisKeeper perspective, it shares the data about your last session, and if it is something that you want to repeat, you can simply tap on the platter to start a new session. Will the TennisKeeper platter shows up at the right time? That is completely between you and the Siri prediction engine.
