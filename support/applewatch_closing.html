TennisKeeper app closing on Apple Watch

A few users have reported that the TennisKeeper Watch app closes in the middle of a workout. Since tennis is an activity that involves sweat and your wrist may be brushing up against the screen of the Apple Watch, there might be a chance that you ended the activity accidentially by a force close. There is also a chance that your Apple Watch is not working properly.

Interestingly, others have also reported similar issues with the Apple Watch fitness app. The TennisKeeper Watch app works in a similar manner as the Apple Watch fitness app. Therefore, this article here may help you. Please follow the step by step outlined here to ensure your Watch is setup properly:

  • Apple Support for fitness app closing


Accidental Force Close

If you are wondering how you can even accidentally force close the app, take a look at this Poemon Go animation to see how to use the side button and Digital Crown. It is unclear how one can accidentally tap those but that is how an app may be force closed.


The Lock Button

To avoid any accidental closing issue, you can tap the Lock button to lock the screen so that nothing but a Force Touch will be able to wake it up for you to properly end and save your workout session.
