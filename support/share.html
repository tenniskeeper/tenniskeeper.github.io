How do I share my scores?

You can share your match scores, notes, tips, lessons learned etc. with your friends or on social media. If you are playing on a league, you can share the players you competed against with your team so that they know what to expect.

There are two ways to share your tennis information:

Option 1:

  1. Go to TennisKeeper > Activities.
  2. Check the date to make sure it is showing the right date. If not, tap the Date button and select the right date.
  3. Swipe left on an existing activity, then click Share.

Option 2:

  1. Go to TennisKeeper > Stats.
  2. Tap the Activities Log
  3. Select the activity you want to share. Tap the Share button on the top right hand corner.

To share a player profile information:

  1. Go to TennisKeeper > Players.
  2. Swipe left on a player, then click Share.
