Notifications on your Apple Watch

During your workout tracking or when you tap End to end your tracking, you may receive notifications from Activity to tell you that you have reached your move or other fitness goals.


This is normal and will not pose a problem to TennisKeeper.
Simply scroll down to the notification, and tap the Dismiss button.


After that, proceed with the Summary page, scroll down and tap Save to save your workout.
