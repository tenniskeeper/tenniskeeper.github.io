Payments

If you choose to subscribe to Live Match Premium, you must designate and provide information about your preferred payment method (e.g. credit card) using your iTunes account. You will immediately be charged for these fees after you tap to purchase Live Match Premium.



Fees and Renewals

You may subscribe to Live Match Premium by month or by year. Monthly subscriptions will automatically renew each month. Yearly subscriptions will automatically renew each year. Your subscription will automatically renew unless auto-release is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period.



Cancellation

You can cancel your Live Match Premium Subscription by going to your iTunes Account Settings after purchase. The cancellation will go into effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will have the same level of access through the remainder of the billing cycle and no refunds will be provided for the remaining billing cycle.
