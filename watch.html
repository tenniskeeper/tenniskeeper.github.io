The Apple Watch is a beautiful and amazing device. If you own one, you can use it to start and save your tennis activities, count your tennis swings and strokes percentages, track distance, steps and heart rate while you are playing.

Track your tennis

Start, pause, continue, stop and save your tennis activity directly from your Apple Watch.



Track your tennis swings

Track your forehand, backhand and other shots percentages. TennisKeeper Watch support uses the Device Motion capabilities from watchOS 3 to detect your swing motion.

Get summary of your workout

Get your steps count, distance covered and heart rate on the tennis court from the Apple Watch. Visualize your strokes breakdown using the interactive dashboard from your iPhone or iPad.
