Effective tennis movement is critical to your success on the tennis court. Proper tennis footwork can help you cover more court and prepare for the next return. Taking many small steps can help you adjust to the ball and enabled you to stay balanced and hit great shots.

Wear your Apple Watch or other Apple Health enabled devices to play tennis, and our interactive dashboards will you show you your tennis footwork.

Compare your Steps

Want to know if you are moving your feet? Compare the number of steps you took for each match.

Select a day to get the week's matches. Tap a circle to get the steps count and distance covered for the selected match.

The Steps Dashboard is only available in the Pro Edition.

Tennis Activity Details

If you are using the Free Edition, you can get the steps count, distance covered, and your average heart rate for each tennis activity in the Activities Log.

Your existing Health Data

TennisKeeper integrates with your Apple Health.

It automatically correlates your existing health data to your tennis events based on the timestamp you logged for your matches.

Your existing health data can be created by your iPhone, Apple Watch or other digital activity trackers such as UP by Jawbone. There is no need to purchase additional tennis specific sensors or trackers.
